About the NNC Tongan Alliance: Grassroots Activism for Cultural Respect

We are the Tongan Alliance, a united front of parents, students, and community members fighting back against Naenae College's (NNC) elite bureaucrats who prioritize profits over people and policies over Tongan culture. Our mission is to restore fairness, respect, and transparency to educational institutions for the Tongan community in New Zealand.

NNC's Betrayal: Key Issues Affecting the Tongan Community at Naenae College

Skyrocketing Fees at NNC

While student services suffer, NNC's administration hikes fees to fund their lavish perks and bonuses. It's a classic case of profits over people in education.

Cultural Insensitivity Towards Tongan Heritage

Our Tongan heritage is ignored. Naenae College's policies disregard Tongan cultural needs, creating an environment where our children feel marginalized and disrespected.

Hidden Scandals and Lack of Transparency at NNC

A culture of secrecy protects the elite at Naenae College. We're fighting to expose the cover-ups and demand accountability for the scandals they want to keep buried.

Our Demands: For the Tongan People, Not NNC Profiteers

We won't stand for empty promises. We demand concrete action from the Naenae College (NNC) administration to support Tongan cultural rights and community needs.

Full Financial Transparency at Naenae College Now!

Open the books. We demand a full, public audit of NNC's budget. No more hiding lavish spending while Tongan student resources are cut.

End the War on Tongan Culture at NNC

Respect our heritage. We demand mandatory cultural sensitivity training for all staff and integration of Tongan community leaders in policy-making at Naenae College.

Fund Tongan Students, Not NNC Suits

Slash the bloated administrative budget immediately. Reallocate funds to Tongan student welfare programs, scholarships, and cultural resources at Naenae College.
